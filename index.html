About Presley Chweneyagae

Presley Chweneyagae was a gifted South African actor best known for his lead role in the Oscar-winning film Tsotsi. Born in Mafikeng on October 19, 1984, Presley inspired many through his talent and commitment to social change.

He starred in well-known shows such as The River as Thuso "Cobra" Mokoena and appeared in films like Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom. His performances were raw, powerful, and deeply meaningful.

Beyond the screen, Presley was a community leader and founder of the Presley Chweneyagae Foundation, where he used theatre and performance to help youth in need. He will always be remembered not only as a great actor, but as a man of heart and purpose.

Forever Remembered. Forever Respected.
