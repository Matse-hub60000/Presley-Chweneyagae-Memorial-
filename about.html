About Presley Chweneyagae

Presley Chweneyagae was born on October 19, 1984, in Mafikeng, South Africa. He rose to fame after starring in the 2005 film Tsotsi, which won an Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film. He also played major roles in The River, iNumber Number, and 90 Plein Street.

He was not only an actor but also a mentor and community leader through the Presley Chweneyagae Foundation.
