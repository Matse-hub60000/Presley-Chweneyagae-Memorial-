Achievements

  • Oscar-Winning Role in Tsotsi (2005): Presley gained international fame for his lead role in the critically acclaimed film Tsotsi, which won the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.
  • Golden Horn Award for Best Actor: For his role as Thuso “Cobra” Mokoena in the television drama The River, Presley won a South African Film and Television Award (SAFTA).
  • Lead Roles in Groundbreaking South African TV Shows: Presley delivered powerful performances in:
    • The River – as Cobra Mokoena
    • iNumber Number – as Shoes Moshoeshoe
    • 90 Plein Street – as Andile Grootboom
  • Roles in Prominent Films:
    • Mandela: Long Walk to Freedom (2013)
    • Zama Zama (2013)
    • State of Violence
  • Over 20 Years of Contribution to the Arts: Presley has been an active contributor to South African theatre and television since his youth, helping shape the country’s modern entertainment landscape.
  • Founder of the Presley Chweneyagae Foundation: Through this foundation, Presley promotes education and personal development through the performing arts, especially for youth in underprivileged areas.
  • National and Global Recognition: He has been recognized both locally and internationally for his talent, leadership, and influence in African storytelling and cinema.
